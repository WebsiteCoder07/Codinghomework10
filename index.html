Why I love photography

Photography lets me freeze a moment and share how I see the world. It combines technical skills like exposure and composition with creativity.

Quick facts

  • Hobby since: 2018
  • Favourite subject: Landscapes & street-life
  • Tools: Camera, tripod, editing software

Steps to improve

  1. Learn camera settings (ISO, aperture, shutter speed).
  2. Practice composition: rule of thirds, leading lines.
  3. Review and edit photos; get feedback.
